What the course covers

The trait that built your business is quietly costing you your marriage. You haven’t been taught the difference yet.

01

Name the inheritance

The operating system you got from your father — the closing instinct, the fix-first reflex, the I-just. Most men have never been told it has a name, and you can’t change what you can’t see.

02

Read the receipts

The cost you’ve already paid — the dinners she stopped suggesting, the rooms she stopped entering, the things your son only tells the other parent. Quiet, compounding, and almost always invisible until someone walks you through them.

03

Run the rep

Small, precise, repeatable. Not therapy-speak. Not a script. The shape of action that actually changes the temperature in your house — built for a man whose nervous system is allergic to vague advice.
